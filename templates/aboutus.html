About Us

Welcome to Safe Girl, where compassion meets action! We are a dedicated team committed to addressing the intricate tapestry of social and environmental challenges in Kenya. Our multifaceted portfolio project aims to create meaningful impact in various critical areas.

Our Mission

Safe Girl is on a mission to make a positive diference in the lives of individuals, communities, and the environment. Through a holistic approach, we strive to foster positive change and empower those facing pressing challenges.

Safe Girl Foundation strives to create a positive ripple efect by providing counseling services, promoting climate awareness, empowering youth through STEM education, supporting GBV victims, enhancing fnancial sustainability, advocating for policy changes, fostering community engagement, and measuring and communicating our impact efectively. We understand the complexity of these challenges and tackle them with a holistic and multifaceted approach.
