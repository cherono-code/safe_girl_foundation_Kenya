Who we Are

Welcome to Safe Girl, a dedicated initiative committed to creating a safer and more empowering world for women and girls in Kenya. At Safe Girl, we believe in the transformative power of community-driven initiatives to address gender-based violence, mental health challenges, and promote holistic well-being.

Safe Girl is not just an organization; it's a community of individuals, advocates, and supporters working collaboratively to make a positive impact. Our initiatives are designed with the input and involvement of the communities we serve, ensuring that the solutions are tailored to their specific needs.

Our Vision

Safe Girl Foundation envisions a world where every girl can thrive, free from the shackles of gender-based violence and inequality. Our vision is rooted in the belief that empowering girls is essential for building resilient communities and fostering societal transformation. We strive to create safe spaces that enable girls to access education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal growth. Through advocacy, awareness campaigns, and comprehensive support programs, we aspire to break down societal barriers, challenge harmful norms, and pave the way for a future where every girl can realize her full potential, contributing to a more just and equitable world. Safe Girl Foundation is committed to championing the rights and well-being of girls, creating lasting impact and empowering the leaders of tomorrow.
