About

The inspiration for this project stems from my personal experiences growing up in Kenya and my deep commitment to mentorship and supporting girls from needy backgrounds. I have witnessed firsthand the significant challenges our communities face, from the lack of mental health resources to the urgent need for environmental sustainability. I believe in the immense potential that lies within our youth, especially girls, if given the right opportunities in STEM. This project is also an integral part of my Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where we strive to develop impactful solutions to real-world problems. By combining my personal dedication and the skills acquired through my education, I aim to create meaningful change and empower the next generation of young women to rise above their circumstances.
